Summary

According to our current App Store privacy label, Tap Match Escape does not collect data from this app. This policy explains what that means in everyday terms and how to reach us with questions.

Apple may still process limited technical information as part of delivering apps from the App Store (for example, download and device compatibility). That processing is governed by Apple’s policies, not by this game alone.

Who this applies to

This policy covers anyone who downloads or plays Tap Match Escape on a supported Apple device (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, or compatible Mac / Apple Vision, as described on the store listing). If you use the game, this page is meant for you.

Information collection

Gameplay is designed so that color matching, level progress, and similar experiences can occur on your device without our servers receiving personal identifiers for ordinary play.

Depending on future updates or optional features (such as analytics or ads, if ever added), practices could change. If that happens, we will update this policy and, where required, the App Store privacy details.

  • We do not ask you to create an in-game account for core play as described on the store page.
  • Crash or diagnostic data sent through Apple’s systems, if any, is subject to Apple’s terms and your device settings.

How we use data

When no personal data is collected by the app itself, there is nothing for us to use for marketing profiles or cross-app tracking through this title. Any local data that exists only on your device (for example, preferences or progress stored locally) stays under your control unless you delete the app or clear that data through system tools.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We would share information only if the law requires it, if we must protect safety or integrity, or if a future service provider strictly needs limited data to operate an optional feature—and only with appropriate agreements and notice where required.

Children

The game is rated 4+ on the App Store. Parents and guardians should review Apple’s Family Sharing and Screen Time settings if a child uses the device. If you believe we have inadvertently received information about a child in a way that concerns you, contact us using the details below.

Your choices & rights

Where applicable law gives you rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data, you may exercise those rights by contacting us. You can also adjust privacy settings on your Apple ID and device to limit analytics, personalized ads (for apps that use Apple’s frameworks), and similar options.

Updates

We may revise this policy when the game or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top will change when we publish an update. Continuing to use the app after changes means you accept the revised policy, except where your consent is required for something new.
