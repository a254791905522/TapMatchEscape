Tap Match Escape

Match colors, dodge obstacles, and escape through themed levels.

Tap Match Escape is a fast-paced casual game for iPhone and iPad where timing and color matching keep you alive. Your character moves on its own—you tap to switch colors and slip through matching gates. Wrong color or bad timing, and it is game over. Simple controls, escalating challenge, and power-ups when you need a breather.

Free to play iOS 15+ English Ages 4+

1 How it plays

  • Your object moves automatically along the path.
  • Colored obstacles appear ahead—watch the pattern.
  • Tap the screen to change your object’s color.
  • Match the obstacle color to pass through safely.
  • Wrong color or mistimed tap ends the run.

2 Why it hooks you

Levels ramp up with trickier sequences so every successful escape feels earned. Smooth motion, satisfying taps, and distinct worlds keep runs fresh whether you play for one minute or twenty.
